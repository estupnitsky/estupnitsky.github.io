All the Dumplings, All the Time

Definition

Dumpling is a broad classification for a dish that consists of small pieces of dough (made from a variety of starch sources) wrapped around a filling.

The dough can be based on bread, flour, or potatoes, and may be filled with meat, fish, cheese, vegetables, fruits, or sweets.

Dumplings may be prepared using a variety of methods, including baking, boiling, frying, simmering, or steaming.
